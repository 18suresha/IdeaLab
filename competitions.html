Here are some competitions IdeaLab members have gone to and/or are planning to participate in. Click on their name to hear a little bit about them.

MIT Blueprint was an absolute blast. From all free food from learning from the best in the business, everyone loved it and wants to go again. The first day was a Learnathon where some of our members learned Web Page Design using HTML/CSS and JavaScript. Other members were part of the Android track and developed their own flappy bird game and learned how to effectively utilize Android Studio Manager.

CAMS CTF was an online Capture the Flag competition, meant for all levels. IdeaLab members learned an abundance from this experience from topics ranging from Forensics, to Exploitation, and even Programming.

hackBCA II is a lot like Blueprint with learning and coding, but it's in New Jersey. Some IdeaLab members are thinking of participating next year.
